Cheryl

49, san diego—california

I am the light
I am above
I am me. I feel
walking, I feel
rain
It is now
there had been a moment when
I lifted the cameras,
I refuse elevators
I have lifted the weight. Now
bound for flying west
there is me
It is the light, standing on land.
I watch it,
Then I fly
The fear is gone
It is confidence and belief
