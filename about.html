How does your frame of reference affect your art?

FRAME OF REFERENCE is "a project demonstrating how our unique perspectives can create equally as valuable art. Sparked from my love of making blackout poems in my free time, I wondered what would happen if I gave a few of my friends the same blank page. What would each of them project onto the piece of paper given their respective nuances? I think this exercise at large emphasizes the way we all respectively view the world differently and always have something to contribute because of it." Try it for yourself here.



THE CREATOR—Mia Magdaraog is an interdisciplinary artist focused on preserving the tangible experience of art in a digital world.
