Grace

20, needham—massachusetts

It is dark
and the lights are wet;
I am above them
I feel the security of solitude,
So long as I can see the lights
It is a little over an hour now
so much time used up
when Time stopped—and Distance too.
—and the question had frightened me.
We are flying west with the night
It is the last light, standing on the last land.
The fear is gone now—

What did you have for breakfast?

How do you think your frame of reference affects your art?

Loaded question. I am very interested in how people see themselves and how they, see the world, as a psychologist. This affects my art because I am showing how I see myself and the world around me through how I think people see themselves and the world around them.
