How do you think your frame of reference affects your art?

I think being a woman affects my art first and foremost. I’m always wanting to create with other women, I’m influenced the most by art made by women, and I often pull from the experience of “girlhood” when creating. I’m also an overthinker, a very diligent observer and I’m extremely sensitive - I think all of those characteristics influence the art I create as well.
