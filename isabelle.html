Isabelle

25, boston—massachusetts

I am over
dry.
solitude,
people walking selfishly triumphant,
left sorrow in other hands.
I left
blue-and-silver burden
at last she proved it.
sophistry said,
are we bound?
—and the question had frightened me.
Most of the night
flashes—so many
The fear is gone now
